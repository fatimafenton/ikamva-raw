A dream come true

The library is a huge inspiration to many of our learners. There were about 41-45 learners in a class, and the library has 6 workstations and 5 tables but we were able to accommodate them in the library, but some of the leaners had to sit on the mat. They also enjoyed doing their research for their tasks, but they were unable to print any of the information found because the library does not have a printer.The library has tables and chairs but with such large amounts of learners in a class more tables and chairs and computers would be greatly appreciated then everyone in class would have computer access and equipping the learners with research techniques would be easier. There are approximately 400 ~Chanice Visagie
