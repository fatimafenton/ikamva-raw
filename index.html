Welcome to Ikamva Ubomi

Ikamva Ubomi is a community based outreach NPO established in December 2011 that aims to: empower kids with employable skills, teach kids a sense of community development, prevent crime, and most importantly give kids direction and opportunity.

Ikamva Ubomi finds projects in communities around Cape Town where our youth can use their newly acquired skills to make a difference. They are taught to give back to the communities that they came from. We actively do projects in pensioners, churches, mosques, schools, etc., that are in need of maintenance or repair. Ikamva Ubomi also uses the help of skilled parents from local communities to teach our youth the basic skills necessary to work on these projects and make an impact. Small businesses assist us by providing finances, tools and other resources needed to complete our projects
